# The 'Dead Internet Theory' Explained
Why most online engagement isn't human
## Abstract
Have you ever swiped through your social media feed and felt like all the posts are the same, repetitive, or oddly anticipated? Imagine that most of what you see — likes, comments, even full posts — aren't from real people, but automated programs.
## Key Points
• 50% of web traffic is non-human (Imperva)
• Rise of AI content tools since 2022-2023
• Bots crowd out genuine human voices
• Impact on business credibility and trust
## Why It Matters
When so much content is synthetic, genuine human connection, trust in brands, and meaningful conversation suffer. For small businesses, that bot noise can drain marketing budgets and erode credibility.
