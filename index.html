EZEKIEL OBEISUN JR. -
CLOUD COMPUTING STUDENT

I'm a results-driven Cloud Computing student at Western Governors University with five years of experience in cybersecurity and IT operations. My career began as a Cybersecurity Intern (CPITS Program) at Trend Micro, where I learned the fundamentals of system protection and security operations. From there, I advanced into a Security Analyst role, gaining hands-on experience identifying threats, strengthening processes, and protecting sensitive information.

In recent years, my focus has shifted toward cloud security and infrastructure. I've worked with platforms such as Amazon Web Services (AWS) and Microsoft Azure, often in high-compliance environments where every detail of how information is managed matters. In these settings, I've learned to take complex requirements and translate them into clear, practical steps that keep systems both secure and reliable.

Now, with AI reshaping technology and security at a rapid pace, my goal is to document my journey — the lessons I learn, the people I meet, and the challenges I face along the way. I believe the industry will only grow more complex, and I want to be prepared for it while maintaining a clear record of my progress. Above all, I aim to approach this path with integrity, ensuring that the work I do leaves a reliable mark and contributes to something lasting.

Every project, lesson, and challenge is a chance to improve.
